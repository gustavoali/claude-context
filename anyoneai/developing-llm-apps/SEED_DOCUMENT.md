## Learning Objectives

1. Gain a solid understanding of Large Language Models (LLMs) and their fundamentals
2. Master prompt engineering to tailor prompts for various applications (creative text generation, translation)
3. Learn information retrieval techniques to efficiently extract relevant information from vast data sets
4. Gain insights into word embeddings (concept for computers to grasp relationships between words)
5. Explore chunking techniques for structuring information for optimal LLM use
6. Get hands-on experience with industry-leading LLM tools: LangChain, Chainlit, OpenAI, Chroma
7. Develop ability to effectively utilize prompt engineering, information retrieval methods, and apply knowledge through industry-standard LLM tools

## Proyecto Final: LLM-based Recruitment Tool

### Descripcion
The current job market demands an efficient system for matching qualified candidates with suitable job openings. Traditional methods often rely on keyword searches and basic filtering, leading to missed opportunities for both employers and potential hires. This project aims to revolutionize recruitment by developing a professional application powered by Large Language Models (LLMs).

### Capacidades
This LLM-based Recruitment Tool will leverage Gemini, LangChain, Chainlit, and Chroma to analyze individual profiles and identify ideal career paths:
- **Go beyond keywords:** Analyze nuances of job descriptions and candidate profiles, identifying underlying skills and requirements
- **Uncover hidden potential:** Recognize transferable skills and career paths not readily apparent in traditional searches
- **Embrace diverse experiences:** Move beyond rigid job titles and qualifications, valuing unique experiences and knowledge
